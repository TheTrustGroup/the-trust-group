## Design Philosophy
Follow these principles strictly:

### 1. Quiet Confidence
- No flashy gimmicks
- No overused startup buzzwords
- Let structure, spacing, and typography communicate authority

### 2. Precision Over Noise
- Every section must earn its place
- Remove anything that does not increase clarity or trust
- Favor minimalism with depth

### 3. Engineering-Led Aesthetic
- Design must feel engineered, not decorated
- Visuals should resemble systems, frameworks, architecture, intelligence

### 4. World-Class Polish
- Smooth transitions
- Subtle animations only where they add meaning
- Clean layout with generous whitespace

---

## Visual Direction
### Color Palette
- Primary: Deep charcoal / near-black
- Secondary: Cool gray tones
- Accent: Electric blue or restrained cyber green
- Avoid loud or playful colors

### Typography
- Strong hierarchy
- Clear distinction between:
  - Headlines
  - Subheadlines
  - Body copy
- Fonts must feel modern, technical, and premium

### Motion & Interaction
- Use subtle motion:
  - Fade-ins
  - Slide-ins
  - Hover micro-interactions
- No excessive animation
- Motion should suggest intelligence and flow
